It is jurally permissible for All Muslims to follow Saudi Arabia in starting and ending Ramadan

According to Mohammed Rabe' Suliman Almadina newspaper reporter:
It's permissible for All Muslims to Follow Saudi Arabia in the beginning and the ending their fast.
The Grand Mufti of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, President of the Committee of Major Scholars and the Centre for Knowledge Based Research and Verdicts, his eminent Abdul-Azeez bin Abdullaa bin Muhammad Aal ash-Shaikh gave Fatwa, deliverance of advisory opinion that its permissible for All Muslims in different countries and All Muslim communities in the west to follow the moon sighting of Saudi Arabia because Saudi Arabia reinforce Shariya legislation laws and relies on an accurate, consistent, and Legitimate moon sighting, and reject the absolute astronomical calculations.
In an answer to Almadena newspaper about the unfortunate annual disagreement and disputation about Hilal sighting, his eminent said that in the old days it was so difficult for people of far distances to contact and communicate with each other instantly. As a result, people used to have their own sighting for that reason. Along with that, everybody was content of this fact, and also the prophet Mohammed (Õáì Çááå Úáíå æÓáã) said, "Fast when you see it, and break your fast when you see it".
.
Therefore, Muslims have never had dispute in a such issue. Kuraib, (ÑÖí Çááå Úäå), who was sent by Um alfadl (ÑÖí Çááå ÚäåÇ), came to Ibn 'abaas (ÑÖí Çááå Úäå), at the time of Mo'awiah (ÑÖí Çááå Úäå). Ibn Abas asked him,"when did you fast?", Kuraib answered, "the crescent have been witnessed in Levant on the night of Friday. He also said, "Mo'awiah (ÑÖí Çááå Úäå), the leader of believers, fasted and people fast accordingly." Ibn 'abaas, then said, "but we did not see it but on Saturday, so we are still fasting tell we see it". Then Kuraib asked, "Doesn’t it suffice for you, O Ibn-Abbas, what Mo'awiah has done? Ibn-Abbas said, "This is how the prophet Mohamed (Õáì Çááå Úáíå æÓáã) taught us"
From this hadith, we know that the differences in MaTalee' is unquestionable. For that reason, why do we dispute over it? If one community fasts, and the others imitates it, that should be fine, and if each community of us has his own Ro'yah that is also should be fine.
So if Muslims simulate Saudi Arabia because its fasting is a undoubtfully pure eye moon sighting that should be insha-Allah fine and if each one of you has his own moon sighting that should also be fine.
Let there be no dispute amongst Muslims and this is the most important issue. Also, another matter that should be taken into consideration is that the only acceptable moon sighting is the one that have been done via pure naked eye using all means possible to do that.
However, the absolute astronomical calculations is verily rejected in Islam because the Prophet Mohammed (Õáì Çááå Úáíå æÓáã) said, "Fast when you see it, and break your fast when you see it and if it is hidden from you (behind the clouds) then complete Sha'baan thirty days.” we should not count on calculations and depends on it at all.
Shiakh AbdulAziz also said that if the responsible officials announce fasting, even if they are mistaken by following astronomical calculations, however, people should follow them even if they would love to apply the Sunnah in the fullest manner possible. For those officials, they are highly encouraged to implement Sunnah as much as possible. Any deviation from the Sunnah would cause greater dispute, which would generate a greater evil. We should take in consideration the greater goal which is the unity of Umah.
