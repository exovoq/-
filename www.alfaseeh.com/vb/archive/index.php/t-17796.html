Rama (disambiguation)
From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
The word Rama can refer to:


Rama within the scriptures of India

Rama, the legendary Indian king regarded as an incarnation of Vishnu
Parasurama, Rama with the axe
Radha-Raman, a name of the avatar, Krishna
Balarama, brother of Krishna
Nityananda Rama, the Vaishnava saint
Specific historical people

"The Rama" (1530 - 1572), Jewish religious teacher Moses Isserles
Kings of Thailand (Thai: สมเด็จรามาธิบดี):
Rama I, the Great (1782-1809), Buddha Yodfa Chulaloke
Rama II (1809-1824), Buddha Loetla Nabhalai
Rama III (1824-1851), Jessadabodindra
Rama IV (1851-1868), Mongkut
Rama V, the Great (1868-1910), Chulalongkorn
Rama VI (1910-1925), Vajiravudh
Rama VII (1925-1935), Prajadhipok
Rama VIII (1935-1946), Ananda Mahidol
Rama IX, the Great (installed 1946), Bhumibol Adulyadej
Edi Rama (born 1964), Albanian politician & artist
Frederick Lenz, a spiritual teacher who used Rama as his teaching name
Fiction

Rama, fictional spacecraft in Rendezvous with Rama and its sequels
RAMA, a 1997 video game based on it.
Rama Kandra, a character in The Matrix Revolutions.
Ethnic group

The Rama (people), of Nicaragua
Geography

Rama, Ramlet or Ramatha in Palestine
Rama Lake near Astore in Pakistan
Rama River, tributary in Herzegovina of the Neretva
Rama, Bosnia and Herzegovina, 12th century duchy figuring in the medieval history of Bosnia and Herzegovina
Rama, Ethiopia, a town in northern Ethiopia.
Rama, Ontario, the town belonging to the Mnjikaning First Nation people, and location of Casino Rama.
